About Your Results

View Results: Shows scored results only - swimmers with calculated points based on their times.

Each ZIP download contains two CSV files:

  • results_by_event.csv - Scored swimmers organized by events (fastest first)
  • results_by_swimmer.csv - Scored events organized by swimmers (alphabetically)

Note: Only results with calculated points are included. Unscored times (DQ, NS, etc.) are excluded.

Need Help? If processing takes longer than expected or you encounter errors, please check your file format and try again. Supported formats: HY3 (HyTek exports), ZIP (containing HY3), XLSX (dryland events).
